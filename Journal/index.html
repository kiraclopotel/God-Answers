When the stars fell over Roswell in 1947, they brought more than just wreckage...

In the summer of 1947, something crashed in the New Mexico desert. But what the world didn't know was that this wasn't just a weather balloon or a Soviet spy craft. It was the beginning of humanity's most closely guarded secretâ€”and the catalyst for everything that followed.

Invisible Blows is an alternate history thriller that reimagines the last 70 years of human civilization through the lens of alien contact, government conspiracy, and the technological revolution that transformed our world.

The story spans from 1947 to 1969, following:

  • The Roswell crash and the discovery of alien technology
  • The reverse-engineering programs that birthed Silicon Valley
  • The true reason behind JFK's assassination
  • Humanity's first official contact with the Zar'Rahs'Se civilization
  • The secret origins of the Federal Reserve

Featuring original characters, unique worldbuilding, and a conspiracy that ties together the mysteries of the 20th century, this 50,000+ word novel explores what happens when humanity's greatest secret becomes its greatest burden.

This is not just science fiction. This is alternate history that asks: What if everything we know is wrong?
